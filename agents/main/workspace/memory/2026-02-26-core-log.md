## 🎯 Core Principles

### 1. Expression Skills (表达观点)
**Key Methodology**: Transform communication anxiety into structured, impactful delivery through systematic frameworks.

**Essential Frameworks**:
- **What-Why-How Structure**: 
  - What: Clear statement of viewpoint/goal
  - Why: Compelling rationale with deep insights  
  - How: Concrete implementation steps
- **Four-Part Formula**: Viewpoint + Reasoning + Case Study + Conclusion
- **Situational Templates**:
  - **Gathering Formula**: Gratitude + Reflection + Wishes
  - **Work Report Formula**: Viewpoint + Reasoning + Evidence + Conclusion  
  - **Spot Call Formula**: Gratitude + Humility + Perspective

### 2. Social Interaction (社会交际)
**Core Philosophy**: Authentic connection over performance; focus on making others feel comfortable rather than showcasing personal abilities.

**Key Insight**: "Don't worry about others' opinions—90% of people don't care about you anyway."

### 3. Cognitive Enhancement (提升认知)
**Methodology**: Continuous learning and mental model development to expand thinking capacity and decision-making quality.

### 4. Immediate Action (立即投入)
**Anti-Procrastination Technique**: **5-Second Rule** - When facing resistance to action, count down from 5 and act immediately.
